# Video 13 — Logo Color Transitions

## Tipo: Brand Identity · Logo Motion
## Duração: 10 segundos
## Materiais: @Image1 (logo-variations.png), @Image2 (brand-mockup.png)

---

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as reference for color variations. @Image2 as brand reference.

0–2s: The Sunny sun-S logo appears in gold #F5A524 on pure black background, centered. It pulses once with a golden glow ring. Camera: static, centered. Sound: deep impact hit.

2–4s: The logo smoothly transitions color — gold morphs to deep amber #E8740C. The glow ring shifts to match. Background remains black. Camera: static. Sound: tonal shift, warm.

4–6s: Logo transitions to warm yellow #FACC15. Brighter, more energetic. Subtle floating particles appear around the logo. Camera: very slight push in. Sound: higher pitch tone, bright.

6–8s: Logo transitions to pure white #FAFAFA. Clean, minimal. Particles fade. Background stays dark. Camera: static. Sound: clean, crystalline tone.

8–10s: Background flips to gold #F5A524 — logo simultaneously turns black #09090B. Perfect inversion. All four small versions appear below in a row (gold, amber, yellow, white, black-on-gold). Text: "Versatile by Design." Camera: slight pull back. Sound: satisfying lock-in chord.

Clean motion graphics, dark to light transitions, smooth color morphing, professional brand identity video, minimal and precise.
```

## Notas
- Cada cor deve ser exatamente a do design system
- Transições devem ser suaves, fluidas — não cortes abruptos
- Ideal para: brandbook, guidelines presentation, social
