# Video 18 — Trade Show Booth

## Tipo: Environmental · Event Branding
## Duração: 12 segundos
## Materiais: @Image1 (brand-mockup.png)

---

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as brand reference.

0–3s: A crowded trade show floor — blurred crowd movement. Camera pushes through the crowd, everything is out of focus and chaotic except one direction. Camera: first-person POV, shallow depth of field, handheld feel. Sound: trade show ambient noise, chatter, music.

3–6s: The crowd parts — revealing the Sunny booth. Dark, sleek, minimal structure standing out from the colorful crowded show floor. Large illuminated golden sun logo on the back wall. A massive LED screen shows the dashboard. Camera: slow push toward booth, rack focus from blurred to sharp. Sound: ambient noise dims, focused attention.

6–9s: Camera enters the booth — detail shots: golden logo backlit on dark panels, tablets showing the dashboard, premium black furniture, golden accent lighting strips on edges. Visitors interact with touchscreens. Camera: tracking shot through booth interior. Sound: clean tech ambient, the booth has its own audio zone.

9–12s: Final hero shot from outside — the booth glows golden against the darker trade show background. "SUNNY — AI-Powered Solar Sales Intelligence" visible on the LED. Camera: pull back to wide, establishing. Sound: warm brand theme resolution.

Photorealistic trade show photography, event lighting, contrast between crowded show floor and premium booth, golden accent illumination, corporate event aesthetic.
```

## Notas
- Rack focus = transição de caos para clareza (conceitual!)
- O booth deve ser visualmente distinto: dark + golden quando todos são coloridos
- Ideal para: event planning, brand activation pitch, investor deck
