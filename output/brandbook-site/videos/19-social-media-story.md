## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as brand reference.

0–2s: Bold white text punches in from the bottom on a pure black background: "70% DOS LEADS". Each word slams in with a screen shake. Camera: static with impact shakes. Sound: heavy bass impacts with each word.

2–4s: Next text line crashes in: "MORREM SEM RESPOSTA." in red #EF4444. The text cracks and crumbles — fragments fall. Camera: slight zoom in. Sound: glass breaking, dramatic.

4–6s: Screen wipes to golden — text appears in black: "E SE NENHUM MORRESSE?" with the Sunny sun logo pulsing. Camera: static, clean. Sound: tone shift to positive, warm golden chime.

6–8s: Quick montage of micro-animations: a chat notification appearing ✅, a calendar filling with meetings 📅, a pipeline graph rising 📈 — all in golden on dark background, fast cuts synchronized to beats. Camera: dynamic cuts, each 0.5s. Sound: electronic beat drops matching cuts.

8–10s: Final frame — Sunny logo centered, tagline: "Nenhum lead morre ao sol." CTA button: "Teste Grátis →" glowing golden. Camera: static. Sound: bass resolution, satisfying.

Vertical 9:16 format, bold kinetic typography, motion graphics, high contrast black and gold, Instagram Story / TikTok ad style, fast-paced, attention-grabbing in first 2 seconds.
```

## Notas
- IMPORTANTE: Formato vertical 9:16 para Stories/Reels/TikTok
- Os primeiros 2s devem ser agressivos para hook
- Ideal para: Instagram Stories ad, TikTok ad, YouTube Shorts
