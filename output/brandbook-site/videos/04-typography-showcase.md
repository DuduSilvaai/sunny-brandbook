# Video 04 — Typography Showcase

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as visual reference.

0–3s: Dark screen. The words "SPACE GROTESK" appear letter by letter in large bold white text, each letter materializing with a golden particle burst. Camera: static, centered. Sound: subtle mechanical clicks with each letter.

3–6s: Text transforms fluidly — the font smoothly morphs from Space Grotesk Bold into Inter Regular, the word changes to "INTER". Letters shift weight and shape organically. Camera: slow push in. Sound: smooth tonal shift, whoosh.

6–9s: Another fluid transition — Inter transforms into "JETBRAINS MONO" with a monospace grid appearing behind, code-like green scan lines sweeping. Camera: slight orbit. Sound: digital typing sounds, terminal beeps.

9–12s: All three typeface names stack vertically — Space Grotesk, Inter, JetBrains Mono — with golden accent bar separating them. The Sunny logo appears at top. Text: "3 Typefaces. 1 System." Camera: pull back to full composition. Sound: resolving ambient chord.

Cinematic quality, dark #09090B background, white and gold #F5A524 text, clean transitions, motion graphics style, premium brand film look.
```

## Notas
- Cada font deve ser claramente distinguível na transição
- Ideal para: brandbook video, design system presentation, social media
