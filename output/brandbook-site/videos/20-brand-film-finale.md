## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as brand reference. @Image2 as logo reference. @Image3 as color palette reference.

0–3s: Pre-dawn darkness. Stars visible. The camera faces east over a silhouetted landscape — solar panels barely visible as dark shapes. Text appears softly: "Enquanto outros dormem..." Camera: static wide, extremely low light. Sound: silence, then a single deep note.

3–6s: The first ray of sunlight pierces the horizon — a thin golden line expanding. The solar panels begin reflecting, waking up. Text: "...nós trabalhamos." Camera: static, natural light change. Sound: orchestral strings entering softly, building.

6–9s: Rapid montage — synced to music beats:
Beat 1: Phone notification "Lead qualificado ✅"
Beat 2: Chat response "Reunião agendada"
Beat 3: Pipeline counter going up
Beat 4: Dashboard showing R$1M milestone
Each beat is a fast cut with golden flash transition.
Camera: quick cuts with golden flash wipes. Sound: powerful electronic beats, each matching a visual.

9–12s: Full sunrise — the sun is now fully above horizon, the solar farm is alive with golden light. Everything is warm, powerful, confident. A person stands among the panels, silhouette facing the sun, arms slightly open. Camera: slow motion wide follow, crane up. Sound: full orchestral + electronic climax.

12–15s: Sun dissolves into Sunny logo. All elements settle — logo centered, tagline below: "Amanheceu, vendeu." followed by "sunny.com.br" and the solar-S icon. Background: pure dark with subtle golden glow. Camera: static hero shot. Sound: final resolving chord, silence.

Master cinematic quality, 2.35:1 widescreen, mixing real documentary footage feel with motion graphics data overlays, golden hour, epic and inspiring, brand anthem film quality. This is the definitive Sunny brand film.
```
