## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as the last frame.

0–3s: Pure black screen. A single golden particle of light appears at the center, pulsating slowly like a distant star. Camera: extreme close-up, static. Sound: deep ambient hum rising in pitch.

3–6s: The particle explodes outward — golden rays extend in all directions forming the triangular sun rays of the Sunny logo. Camera: slow push in tracking the expansion. Light streaks radiate from center. Sound: powerful woosh with metallic resonance.

6–8s: The letter "S" materializes at the center of the sun formation, golden and bold, locking into position with a final flash. Camera: slight pull back to reveal full logo mark. Sound: bass impact hit, reverb tail.

8–10s: The full Sunny logo settles with a subtle golden glow pulse. Text "Sunny" fades in below in Space Grotesk typeface. Camera: static, centered. Sound: warm ambient pad resolving to silence.

Cinematic quality, dark background #09090B, golden color #F5A524, shallow depth of field, particle effects, lens flare on logo completion. 2.35:1 widescreen aspect ratio.
```
