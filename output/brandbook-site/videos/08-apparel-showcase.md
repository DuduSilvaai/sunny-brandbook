# Video 08 — Apparel Showcase

## Tipo: Brand Activation · Product Photography
## Duração: 12 segundos
## Materiais: @Image1 (mockups-apparel.png), @Image2 (brand-mockup.png)

---

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as visual reference for products. @Image2 as brand reference.

0–3s: Pure black. A spotlight slowly illuminates a premium black t-shirt floating and rotating slowly mid-air. The golden Sunny sun logo on the chest catches light with a metallic sheen. Camera: slow orbit around the t-shirt. Sound: ambient luxury brand music, soft bass.

3–5s: Smooth transition — the t-shirt morphs/transforms into a dark charcoal hoodie with golden "Sunny" text across the chest. Same floating rotation. Camera: continuous orbit, seamless. Sound: fabric swoosh transition.

5–7s: Another smooth morph — hoodie becomes a sleek black snapback cap. The embroidered golden sun logo catches directional light. Camera: tilt down to cap detail. Sound: another fabric swoosh.

7–9s: Cap transforms into a black bomber jacket. Front zip reflects golden light. Small logo on chest gleams. Camera: slow pull back. Sound: premium material sound.

9–12s: All four items appear arranged in a lifestyle flat-lay composition on dark surface — viewed from above. Golden accent lighting from one corner. Sunny logo and text: "Wear the Future." Camera: bird's eye, static. Sound: resolving ambient chord with bass.

Photorealistic quality, studio product photography lighting, dark premium aesthetic, golden accent #F5A524, luxury fashion brand feel, smooth morphing transitions.
```

## Notas
- Transições morph entre peças = diferencial visual
- Cada peça deve mostrar o logo claramente
- Ideal para: e-commerce, social media, brand activation
