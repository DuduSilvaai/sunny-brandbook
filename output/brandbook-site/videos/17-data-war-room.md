## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as product reference. @Image2 as brand reference.

0–3s: Dark room with multiple screens — a "Torre de Comando" visualization. One central large screen flanked by four monitors. All screens off, dark. A hand reaches for a desk. Camera: wide establishing shot, dark thriller lighting. Sound: silence, then a power-up hum.

3–6s: All screens illuminate simultaneously — the Sunny dashboard fills every screen. Golden data streams across: real-time pipeline, lead map of Brazil with pulsing dots, conversion funnels, chat conversations. Camera: slow push forward toward the central screen. Sound: electronic startup sequence, data stream sounds.

6–10s: Camera pushes past screens into a 3D holographic visualization — the lead pipeline becomes a golden 3D river flowing through space. Numbers float: "340 leads · 89% response rate · R$2.1M pipeline." Camera: one-take flight through 3D data space. Sound: epic electronic score building.

10–13s: Camera pulls back — we see the silhouette of a person sitting in front of all screens, the golden light illuminating them from behind. They lean forward, confident. Camera: dramatic low angle, hero shot. Sound: building to climax.

13–15s: Screen transitions to Sunny logo on dark. Text: "Torre de Comando — O integrador do futuro senta aqui." Camera: static. Sound: powerful resolution, bass.

Cinematic tech thriller aesthetic, dark room with screen glow, golden accent lighting, Bloomberg Terminal meets Mission Control, intense and powerful atmosphere.
```
