# Video 12 — Solar Farm Aerial

## Tipo: Brand Atmosphere · Cinematic B-Roll
## Duração: 15 segundos
## Materiais: @Image1 (wide aerial photo of solar panels at sunset)

---

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as the first frame.

0–5s: Breathtaking aerial view of an enormous solar farm at golden hour. Camera starts high and descends slowly — thousands of solar panels reflecting orange sky, creating a geometric sea of gold and black. Camera: drone descent, bird's eye transitioning to 45-degree angle. Sound: wind at altitude, deep ambient bass.

5–10s: Camera reaches panel level — skimming just above the surface. Reflections of clouds move across panels. The horizon glows with deep amber sunset. Camera: low-altitude forward tracking shot at high speed, panels blurring beneath. Sound: accelerating whoosh, building cinematic strings.

10–13s: Camera tilts up toward the setting sun — massive golden orb filling the frame. Lens flare cascades across the image. Camera: dramatic tilt up, slow motion. Sound: orchestral crescendo, full swell.

13–15s: Sun dissolves into the Sunny logo — same golden circle, same radiating energy. Text fades in: "Powered by the Sun. Driven by Intelligence." Camera: static hero shot. Sound: powerful resolution, single sustained note.

Epic cinematic quality, 2.35:1 widescreen, drone photography, golden hour, lens flares, epic and grand atmosphere, 24fps film look, high dynamic range.
```

## Notas
- Transição sol real → logo é a conexão conceitual central
- B-roll puro — pode ser usado sob narração
- Ideal para: homepage background video, apresentações, investor deck
