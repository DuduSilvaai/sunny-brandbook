# Video 06 — Manifesto Film

## Tipo: Brand Manifesto · Cinematic Short
## Duração: 15 segundos
## Materiais: @Image1 (brand-mockup.png), @Image2 (solar farm landscape)

---

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1's brand identity as style reference.

0–3s: Aerial drone shot over a massive solar farm at dawn. Sun is just below the horizon — sky shifts from deep purple to amber. Solar panels stretch infinitely. Camera: slow crane up revealing scale. Sound: wind ambience, deep bass rumble building.

3–6s: Cut to close-up — a phone screen shows "Novo Lead: Carlos M. — 3 segundos atrás". A golden notification pulse. Camera: extreme close-up on phone screen glowing in the dawn light. Sound: notification chime, heartbeat rhythm.

6–9s: Wide shot — the sun breaks the horizon, golden light floods across the solar farm. Everything transforms from shadow to gold. Text appears: "Enquanto o sol nasce..." Camera: static wide, natural lighting shift. Sound: orchestral swell beginning.

9–12s: Rapid montage — pipeline filling with orange nodes, conversation bubbles appearing, calendar filling with meetings. All visualized as golden light paths connecting dots on a dark map. Camera: dynamic cuts, whip pans between data. Sound: building to climax.

12–15s: Final hero shot — the Sunny sun logo emerges from the actual sun in the landscape. Text: "Nenhum lead morre ao sol." Sunny brand lockup below. Camera: slow push in to sun. Sound: full orchestral resolution, powerful.

Cinematic quality, 2.35:1 widescreen, golden hour photography, epic and grand atmosphere, documentary meets brand film.
```

## Notas
- O filme manifesto combina paisagem real com dados/tech
- Tensão narrativa: dawn = oportunidade, sol = Sunny
- Ideal para: vídeo institucional, homepage, investor deck
