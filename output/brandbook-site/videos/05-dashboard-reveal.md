## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as the hero product. @Image2 as reference for brand colors.

0–3s: A sleek dark MacBook Pro floats on pure black background, slightly angled. Screen is off. Camera: slow orbit from left side, soft ambient lighting. Sound: subtle tech hum, anticipation.

3–6s: The MacBook screen illuminates — the Sunny dashboard appears with orange charts, pipeline view, and real-time data. Golden light emanates from the screen, casting warm reflection. Camera: push in toward screen, passing the bezel into the UI. Sound: power-on chime, data flow sounds.

6–10s: Camera is now inside the dashboard — flying through 3D data visualizations. Orange bar charts rise from the surface, pipeline nodes connect with golden light trails, a conversion funnel glows. Numbers tick upward rapidly. Camera: one-take forward tracking through data landscape. Sound: electronic pulse rhythm, escalating.

10–13s: Camera pulls back out of the screen. An iPhone appears floating next to the MacBook showing the mobile version. A notification pops: "☀️ Novo lead qualificado — Maria S." Camera: pull back to reveal both devices. Sound: notification ping, satisfying.

13–15s: Both devices settle. Sunny logo glows between them. Tagline: "Torre de Comando. Sempre On." Camera: static hero shot. Sound: warm ambient resolution.

Cinematic quality, dark studio lighting, product photography aesthetic, golden accent lighting #F5A524-on-#09090B, Apple-quality product showcase feel.
```
