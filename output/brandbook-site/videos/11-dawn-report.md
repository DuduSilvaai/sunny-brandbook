## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as product reference. @Image2 as brand identity.

0–2s: A phone screen on a bedside table, dark room at dawn. The screen illuminates at exactly 8:00 AM — golden Sunny logo appears with notification: "☀️ Dawn Report — Bom dia, Commander." Camera: close-up on phone, rack focus from blurred foreground. Sound: gentle alarm chime, warm.

2–5s: The phone screen expands — we see the Dawn Report: "3 reuniões agendadas ontem · 12 leads qualificados · R$340K pipeline ativo." Numbers animate upward with golden counters. Camera: push into phone screen. Sound: data ticking sounds, positive.

5–8s: Dashboard transitions to a map view — golden dots pulsing across Brazil showing active leads. A rising sun graphic overlays the map from east. Camera: slow pull back. Sound: orchestral morning theme, inspiring.

8–10s: Screen dims. Text overlay: "Dawn Report — Seu briefing diário. Antes do café." Sunny logo. Camera: static. Sound: notification resolved, ambient.

Cinematic quality, warm morning lighting, phone UI design, golden accents, intimate but professional, warm and inspiring mood.
```
