```
0–3s: Modern office lobby, dark stone walls. Camera enters through glass doors — slow walking tracking shot, first-person POV. The space is premium: polished concrete, dark marble, minimal furniture. Sound: footsteps on marble, ambient lobby echo.

3–6s: Camera pans right revealing a large wall-mounted sign — the Sunny sun-S logo in brushed gold metal, backlit with warm amber LEDs creating a halo effect. "Sunny" in dimensional gold letters beside it. Camera: pan and slow push toward the sign. Sound: subtle awe moment, ambient shift to warmer tone.

6–8s: Close-up on the logo sign — detail of brushed metal texture, light catching the edges, the warm backlight casting golden rays onto the dark wall. Camera: extreme close-up, shallow depth of field. Sound: warm ambient hum.

8–10s: Pull back to wide shot. The full lobby composition — the golden sign is the focal point, a minimalist reception desk below, modern chairs. Clean, premium, powerful. Text: "Headquarters — São Paulo." Camera: wide establishing shot. Sound: resolving ambient chord.

Photorealistic architectural visualization, luxury office design, dark materials, golden accent lighting, premium corporate branding, shallow depth of field.
```
