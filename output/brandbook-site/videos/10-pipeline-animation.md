```
@Image1 as product reference.

0–3s: Dark 3D space. A single glowing golden dot appears — labeled "Lead". It pulses with energy. Camera: close-up on the dot. Sound: electronic pulse, anticipation.

3–6s: The dot begins moving right along a golden light trail. It passes through gates labeled "Qualificação" → "Contato" → "Visita". At each gate, the dot brightens and grows. More dots appear behind, forming a stream. Camera: tracking follow shot alongside the lead journey. Sound: progressive electronic beats, each gate adding a layer.

6–9s: The stream of dots reaches "Proposta" → "Fechamento". The final gate erupts in golden particles — successful conversion. A counter appears: "+R$85.000". Camera: dynamic push through the final gate with particle explosion. Sound: epic impact, success celebration tone.

9–12s: Pull back to reveal the entire pipeline as a beautiful 3D flow visualization — hundreds of dots flowing through stages, golden light trails connecting everything. Text overlay: "Pipeline Solar — Visão Total." Sunny logo appears. Camera: crane up revealing full pipeline map. Sound: ambient resolution, confidence.

Cinematic data visualization, dark background, golden light trails #F5A524, 3D floating UI elements, holographic tech aesthetic, clean motion graphics.
```
