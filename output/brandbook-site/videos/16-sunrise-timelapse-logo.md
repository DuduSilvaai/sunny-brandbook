# Video 16 — Sunrise Timelapse Logo

## Tipo: Nature + Brand · Atmospheric
## Duração: 10 segundos
## Materiais: @Image1 (brand-mockup.png logo)

---

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as the last frame.

0–4s: Timelapse of a real sunrise over a Brazilian cityscape. Night transitions to dawn — stars fade, sky shifts from deep navy through purple, magenta, orange to bright gold. City lights below slowly go out. Camera: static tripod, wide landscape. Sound: time-stretched ambient sounds, birds accelerating.

4–7s: The sun breaks the horizon — a blazing golden orb growing. As it rises, the sun's shape begins to subtly morph — the outline of the Sunny logo rays appear around the sun disc. Camera: static with slight push in toward sun. Sound: orchestral sunrise theme, warmth building.

7–10s: The sun freezes mid-rise — now it IS the Sunny logo. The city below fades to dark, the sky simplifies to a gradient. The natural sun has become the brand logo. Text: "Sunny. Amanheceu, vendeu." Camera: static, clean composition. Sound: final chord, powerful and warm.

Timelapse photography quality, real sunrise colors, natural-to-brand transition, 2.35:1 widescreen, golden hour, epic transformation.
```

## Notas
- Conceito: sol real → logo é a metáfora definitiva
- Transição deve ser sutil, quase mágica
- Ideal para: brand film opener, social media, homepage
