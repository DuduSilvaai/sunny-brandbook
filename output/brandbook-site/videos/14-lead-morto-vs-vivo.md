## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as brand reference for final frame.

0–3s: Left side of split screen — a dark, cold, desaturated office. A phone screen shows "5 notificações não lidas — 48h atrás". The notifications are gray, dead. Text overlay in red: "SEM SUNNY". Camera: static, cold lighting. Sound: flat beep, clinical, empty.

3–6s: Right side illuminates — warm, golden-lit office. Same phone shows "0 leads pendentes ✅". Golden notifications with checkmarks. Text overlay in gold: "COM SUNNY". Camera: static, warm golden lighting. Sound: bright chime, energy.

6–9s: Left side: a CRM screen shows empty pipeline, cobwebs visual effect. Numbers declining: "R$0 · 0 reuniões · 0 respostas." Camera: slow zoom on dead pipeline. Sound: descending sad tone.

9–12s: Right side: Sunny dashboard blazes with activity — pipeline full of golden dots moving, "R$850K · 47 reuniões · 100% respondido." Camera: same slow zoom but on golden, alive pipeline. Sound: ascending triumphant tone.

12–15s: Split screen collapses — right side wins, expanding full screen. Sunny logo center. Text: "Qual lado você escolhe?" then "Nenhum lead morre ao sol." Camera: right side expanding. Sound: impactful bass drop, resolution.

Split screen composition, cinematic quality, extreme contrast — cold blue/gray vs warm gold, problem/solution narrative, dark #09090B base.
```

## Notas
- Contraste visual extremo é essencial
- "SEM vs COM" = prova social instantânea
- Ideal para: social media ad, Google Ads video, landing page
