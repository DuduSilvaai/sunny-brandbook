# Video 02 — Hero Tagline

## Tipo: Brand Manifesto · Kinetic Typography
## Duração: 15 segundos
## Materiais: @Image1 (logo Sunny), @Image2 (solar landscape, sunrise over solar panels)

---

## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1's logo as the subject. Scene references @Image2.

0–4s: Wide establishing shot — a vast solar farm at golden hour, thousands of panels stretching to the horizon. Camera: slow drone push forward, low altitude flyover. Light catches panel surfaces creating reflective streaks. Sound: wind ambience, subtle musical tension building.

4–8s: Camera accelerates forward dramatically — panels blur beneath as speed increases. Bold white text "NENHUM LEAD" appears floating mid-air, passing through camera. Then "MORRE" appears. Camera: fast track forward with motion blur. Sound: accelerating whoosh, building energy.

8–12s: Camera suddenly pulls up vertically into the golden sky — the sun dominates the frame. Text "AO SOL." appears dramatically against the blazing sun. Camera: rapid tilt up into sun flare. Sound: powerful bass crescendo, cinematic impact.

12–15s: The Sunny logo materializes from the sun's center, golden and luminous. Tagline settles below: "AI-Powered Solar Sales Intelligence." Camera: static, centered hero shot. Sound: resolving chord, warm ambient conclusion.

Cinematic quality, golden hour lighting, 2.35:1 widescreen, epic and grand atmosphere, high saturation warm tones, lens flares.
```

## Notas
- Usar imagem de painel solar como background
- Kinetic typography deve ser bold, Space Grotesk
- Ideal para: homepage hero video, social media header
