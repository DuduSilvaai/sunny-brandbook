## Prompt Seedance 2.0

```
@Image1 as reference for color palette.

0–3s: Black screen. A horizontal line of liquid gold #F5A524 streams across the center of frame, flowing organically like molten metal. Camera: static, centered. Sound: liquid metallic flowing sound.

3–5s: The gold stream splits — one branch turns warm yellow #FACC15, another deepens to amber #E8740C. They flow in parallel, creating a three-lane river of brand colors. Camera: slow push in. Sound: harmonic tones matching each color.

5–8s: From above, a stream of pure white #FAFAFA descends, and from below, deep black #09090B rises — all five colors converge at center forming a radial pattern. Camera: overhead bird's eye view rotating slowly. Sound: crescendo building.

8–10s: The colors solidify into clean rectangular swatches arranged in a horizontal bar — the exact Sunny color palette. Each swatch has its hex code fading in below. Camera: slow pull back to reveal full palette. Sound: satisfying lock-in click.

10–12s: The palette floats on the dark background, Sunny logo appears above. Text: "Solar Palette — Command Black to Solar Flare." Camera: static. Sound: ambient resolution.

Cinematic quality, dark background, fluid dynamics simulation, metallic sheen on color streams, professional brand video quality.
```

## Notas
- As cores devem ser exatas: #F5A524, #E8740C, #FACC15, #FAFAFA, #09090B
- Movimento orgânico/líquido = premium feel
- Ideal para: brandbook video section, design system presentation
